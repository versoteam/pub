• What is versocash ?

    Versocash is a free service that lets you earn crypto back on goods, services, and travel through partnerships with thousands of brands, leading retailers and online services.

    • you can earn by shopping for yourself using our affiliate links or our browser extension.
    • you can earn from your website visitors if a purchase occurs. simply by placing our script to automatically convert your website links into affiliate links.

    Everything you earn will be transferred to the same wallet you used to access our service and in the native currency of your network/blockchain.

  • When/Where will my earnings be paid?

    Your earnings will automatically be transferred to your wallet at the end of each month.
    please note:

    • only approved earnings are transferred.
    • you'll get paid after passing the minimum payout (this may vary depending on your network/blockchain).
    • you'll receive your earnings in the network's native currency (eg: on ethereum you'll receive ether).
  • How does versocash make money?

    We have partnered with lots of advertisers/stores that pay us if we send shoppers their way. so after clicking through from our site, if a purchase occurs the store will give us a commission for sending you to their website, and at the end, we split the profit with you.

  • What payout methods are available?

    Everything you earn will be transferred to the same wallet you used to access our service. there are no other payout methods.

  • Can i use a password for my account?

    No, you don't need a password on versocash. a trustless authentication is in your favor to maintain the anonymity of your network/blockchain usage.

  • Can i use versocash to directly support a charity?

    Yes, if you would like to support a charity please verify if the organization accepts cryptocurrency donations and you can use their wallet address to access versocash. we automatically transfer the earnings no matter who's the owner of the wallet used.
